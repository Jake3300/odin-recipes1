Chicken Parm

About this recipe

It's fatty and has that perfect crispy AND melt in your mouth texture. This recipe has everything you want in chicken parm, full stop-fried cutlets shrieking with crispiness, a perfect blend of mozzarella and parm, and a sauce that is full of life with basil and just enough sweetness.

Ingredients

Marinara Sauce

  • 3 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 (28-oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 large sprigs basil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Chicken

  • 1 c. panko bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 c. finely grated parmesan, divided
  • 3/4 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 (1/4"-thick) boneless, skinless chicken cutlets (about 1 1/2 lb. total)
  • 1 c. shredded mozzarella
  • 1 c. vegetable or canola oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Chopped fresh basil, for serving
